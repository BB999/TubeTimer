TubeTimer - Control Your YouTube Time

Are you spending too much time on YouTube? TubeTimer helps you take control of your daily YouTube usage with a simple and effective time limit feature.

FEATURES:
- Set daily time limits (e.g., 2 hours per day)
- Real-time remaining time display
- Automatic blocking when limit is reached
- Daily reset at midnight
- Quick settings from popup
- Detailed statistics in options page
- English and Japanese language support

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Set your daily YouTube time limit
2. Browse YouTube as usual
3. TubeTimer tracks your usage in the background
4. When you reach your limit, YouTube is blocked until midnight

PERFECT FOR:
- Students who want to focus on studying
- Professionals looking to boost productivity
- Parents managing children's screen time
- Anyone wanting to reduce YouTube addiction

PRIVACY:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is collected or sent to external servers
- No account required
