# Privacy Policy for TubeTimer

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

## Overview

TubeTimer is a Chrome extension that helps users limit their daily YouTube usage time. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**TubeTimer does NOT collect any personal data.**

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

- **Settings**: Your daily time limit preferences and enabled/disabled status
- **Usage Data**: Today's YouTube usage time (in seconds) and last reset date
- **Language Preference**: Your selected display language (English or Japanese)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Collect personal information
- Track your browsing history
- Send data to external servers
- Share any data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## Permissions

TubeTimer requires the following permissions:

- **storage**: To save your settings and usage data locally
- **alarms**: To reset daily usage at midnight
- **tabs**: To detect when YouTube tabs are active
- **host_permissions (youtube.com)**: To track time spent on YouTube

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the extension.
